Lasagna Recipe Flow

Description

This is a simple and delicious lasagna recipe that you can make at home.

BigBaba Lasagna is the best you can ever get and now you can make it from the comfort of your home at any time you want

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Cut the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef until browned.
  3. Drain the excess fat and add the sauce.
  4. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  5. Layer the noodles, meat sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
