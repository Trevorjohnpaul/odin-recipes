Lasagna Recipe Flow

Description

This is a simple and delicious lasagna recipe that you can make at home.

BigBaba Lasagna is the best you can ever get and now you can make it from the comfort of your home at any time you want

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Cut the chicken breast into bite-sized pieces.
  2. In a bowl, mix the flour, salt, and pepper.
  3. Dredge the chicken pieces in the flour mixture.
  4. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  5. Fry the chicken pieces until golden brown and cooked through.
