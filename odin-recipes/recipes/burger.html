Burger Recipe Flow

Description

This is a simple and delicious burger recipe that you can make at home.

BigBaba Burger is the best you can ever get and now you can make it from the comfort of your home at any time you want

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices of cheese
  • Lettuce, tomato, and onion

Instructions

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Form the ground beef into 4 patties.
  3. Place the patties on the grill and cook for 5-7 minutes on each side.
  4. Place a slice of cheese on each patty during the last minute of cooking.
  5. Toast the buns on the grill for 30 seconds.
  6. Assemble the burgers with lettuce, tomato, and onion.
